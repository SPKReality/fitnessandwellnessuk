Common queries and questions


Common questions asked, if your question is not here contact us using our form!


What makes your site different from other fitness platforms?

Our platform combines expertly crafted workout videos, tailored stretching routines, and personalized fitness plans, all designed to fit your lifestyle and goals.

Is there a membership fee to access the content?

We offer both free and premium memberships. Free users can access a selection of videos, while premium members enjoy the full library, downloadable content, and additional features.

Do I need to create an account to use the site?

You are not required to however, creating an account allows you to personalize your experience, save progress, and access tailored recommendations.

Is the site mobile-friendly or available as an app?

Yes, our platform is fully optimized for mobile use, however we do not offer a app at this point in time.

How often is new content added to the platform?

We update our page weekly with fresh workout videos, stretching techniques, and expert fitness tips.

Can I interact with trainers or get support through the site?

Yes, our premium members have access to trainer Q&A sessions, and everyone can reach out to our support team for assistance.

What technical requirements are needed to stream videos?

Our videos stream smoothly on most devices with an internet connection. For optimal performance, we recommend the latest version of your browser.

How can I cancel or upgrade my membership?

You can easily manage your membership through your account settings. Changes take effect immediately, and no hidden fees apply.

What customer support options are available?

Our support team is available via email and a detailed help center to answer any questions you may have.

Can I combine your stretching routines with other workouts?

Definitely, Our stretching routines are designed to complement any workout, whether it’s strength training, cardio, or yoga.
